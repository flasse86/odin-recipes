Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti carbonara combines crispy pancetta, parmesan cheese, and a creamy sauce made from simple ingredients you likely have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Pasta
  • Spice and seasonings
  1. Whish eggs, parmesan cheese and back pepper in a bowl and site aside
  2. Cook pasta until it is al dente. Do not rinse the pasta
  3. Quickly add the egg mixture to the hot cooked pasta and toss to combine
  4. Top with a sprinkle of parmesen cheese and a little chooped parsley for garnish!
